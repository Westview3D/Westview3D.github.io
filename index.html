Who we are

We are an ASB recognized club that focuses on giving students experience with professional 3D graphics software

Our ASB page

What softwares do we teach

For the 2021-2022 school year, we will be teaching blender, a free and open source 3D modeling and rendering software and Unreal Engine, one of the most advanced game engines used by professional game developers.

blender Unreal Engine

How can I join

To join, email westview3d@gmail.com. No experience is required to join the club. You can also join our discord server which is where all of out important communication happens.

Sample work

These are some renders made by club members. All of these were made from scratch and completely computer generated.

Nature render

This was a scene imagined and soon brought to life with blender. Nearly all the assets were modeled by hand except for the rock and some weeds which were photoscanned.

Watch render

This was a render of a members watch. It was completely modeled in blender with mostly procedural materials.

Water simulation

This was our first experience with blender's mantaflow fluid simulation engine. Although it fried our computers, the end result was worth it.

Lamborghini Terzio Milenio Studio Render

Using the widely available Lamborghini Terzio Milenio model, this render was made in blender.
